# 30-Day Growth Validation — Phase 17

Phase 17 measures whether the Phase 15 adaptive strategy improves meaningful outcomes. It does not manufacture a conclusion and it never changes publishing settings automatically.

## Study lifecycle

Open `/validation` and start the study once the current strategy is ready to remain stable. DevPulse stores:

1. The previous 30 days as a comparison baseline.
2. The exact cadence, quality, novelty, cooldown, and posting-window settings.
3. A captured baseline plus pending checkpoints for days 7, 14, 21, and 30.
4. Cumulative X and LinkedIn metrics and recommendations at every captured boundary.

The next checkpoint button stays disabled until its scheduled time. Historical checkpoint evidence is retained for auditability.

Record current X and LinkedIn follower checkpoints in Analytics at the beginning of the study. A first observation entered just after the start is retained as the follower baseline; later follower values are never inferred from engagement.

## Decision rules

- At least 80% of published platform posts should have comparable metrics.
- A platform needs at least five measured posts and 500 impressions before quality tuning is suggested.
- Cadence is evaluated at a conservative 70% adherence floor.
- Follower growth comes only from explicit follower checkpoints.
- Change one variable at a time; prefer controlled experiments for hooks or formats.

If coverage or sample size is weak, the correct recommendation is to collect evidence. At day 30, the study records a conclusion and leaves any settings change for explicit human review.
